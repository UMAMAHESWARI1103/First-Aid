FIRST AID AND EMERGENCY TREATMENT GUIDE

About

    First Aid is the immediate care or sick or injured person gets.In some cases, it may be the 
    only care person needs.In others,first Aid is a way to prevent a person's condition from 
    worsening and keep them alive until paramedies arrive or they are taken to the hospital.
    First aid refers to medical attention that is usually administered immediately after 
    the injury occcurs and at the location where it occured.
